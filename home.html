    About me

    Hello there! I am Muhammad, and if I were to describe myself in one hyphenated word, it would be problem-solver. To keep myself motivated, I give myself tasks and jump right outside my comfort zone.

    I am a third-year Software Engineering student at the University of Queensland and delved into many projects from development of the Atlantis Sinks, to a flood visualisation and simulation application Floodit. Along the way, I have learnt many skills and gained profficiency in many programming lanaguages like Python, Matlab, Java, C, Haskell and familiarised myself with development applications/frameworks like Unity and LibGdx.

    I have also a designer background with freelance graphic design work and built upon (alongside my software development) a designer portfolio.

    In my freetime, I like videogaming, solving difficult maths problems, coding and starting new projects over a heap of unfinished projects.
